About

A hackathon is any event of any duration where people come together to solve problems. Hacking is creative problem solving. (It does not have to be about technology.)Participants typically form groups of about 2-5 individuals, take out their laptops , and dive into problems.

This time on popular demand IIIT-A is organising mini hacakthon for its students, a 20 hour long event. The hackathon would inspire you to imagine, design, code and create. So it time to show the magiic in your fingers and create the project you have always thought of, or discussed with your friend on the late night intellectual tea breaks, or anything random which is too crazy to be ignored.
